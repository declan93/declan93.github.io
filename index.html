Declan Bennett

Computational Biologist at St. Jude Children's Research Hospital, developing machine learning approaches to identify therapeutic vulnerabilities in pediatric cancers.

Research Focus

Leveraging computational methods to accelerate precision medicine in pediatric oncology

Pediatric Cancer Dependencies

Leading computational analysis for the Pediatric Dependency Map (PedDep), identifying genetic vulnerabilities and drug targets in ~300 pediatric cancer cell lines.

ALTitude: Telomere Maintenance

Developed machine learning models to predict Alternative Lengthening of Telomeres (ALT) status, revealing therapeutic vulnerabilities like SMARCAL1 in ALT+ cancers.

Genomics & GWAS

Experience with large-scale genomic analysis (UK Biobank, TCGA, GTEx) and developing methods to improve GWAS statistical power using polygenic scores.
